Ingredients

  • Flour - 700g
  • Water - 500g
  • Active Dry Yeast - 9g
  • Salt - 15g

Steps

  1. Mix water and yeast.
  2. Leave it for a couple of minutes.
  3. If water starts bubbling, yeast is alive.
  4. Add the remaining ingredients.
  5. Mix for 5 minutes.
  6. Then cover and leave it for 10-15 five.
  7. Mix till nice and smooth.
  8. Put the evenly portioned dough in fridge for 24 to 48 hours.
